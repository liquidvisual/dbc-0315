    Superannuation

    As from 1 July 2016, accountants are prohibited by legislation to provide advice on Self Managed Superannuation Funds unless they are licensed or become an authorised representative of another license holder.

    DB+Co hold a Limited Australian Financial Services license no. 485764 through its associated entity, DB+Co SMSF Advisory Pty Ltd, enabling the practice to provide advice in relation to Superannuation, including Self Managed Superannuation Funds.
